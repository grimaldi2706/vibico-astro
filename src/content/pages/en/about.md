# About VIBICO

VIBICO was born from the love for cycle touring and the beauty of Colombian landscapes.

## Our Mission

To promote the use of bicycles as a means of sustainable transport and tourism, connecting people with nature and the culture of Colombia.

## The Team

We are a group of friends, cyclists, and adventurers who decided to share our passion with the world.
