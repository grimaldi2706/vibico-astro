# Welcome to VIBICO

We are a community passionate about traveling Colombia by bicycle. Our mission is to document and share the best routes, tips, and experiences for cycle travelers.

## What will you find here?

- **Detailed Routes**: Maps, altimetry, and points of interest.
- **Travel Tips**: What to pack, where to sleep, and how to prepare.
- **Stories**: Tales from other travelers to inspire you.

Join the adventure and discover Colombia on two wheels!
