## 🏞️ Welcome to VIBICO
{/*
**VIBICO** was born on **September 11, 2015** in Bogotá.\
In **2023**, it was formally established as a **foundation** before the Bogotá Chamber of Commerce.
*/}
We are a community passionate about exploring Colombia by bicycle. Our mission is to support bike travelers in Colombia and other countries.

We offer practical resources, maps, contacts, useful apps, real travel experiences, and a space for radio-style sharing. Everything is designed to promote respect, mutual help, and responsible access to bicycle touring.

## 📌 What will you find here?

- 🛣️ **Detailed Routes**: Maps, elevation profiles, and points of interest.
- 📜 **Travel Tips**: What to bring, where to sleep, and how to prepare.
- 📞 **Emergency Numbers**: Key contacts for assistance during your trip.
- 📚 **Traveler Information**: Border crossings, paperwork, travel logistics.
- 📝 **Stories**: Inspiring stories from other travelers.
